Are there restrictions during the holy month of Ramadan
<-answer->
Please note the following general guidelines during the Holy month of Ramadan. • It is not allowed to eat, drink, chew gum or smoke in public during daylight hours, including inside a car • It is respectful and polite to dress more conservatively during Ramadan – shoulders and legs should be covered • Most businesses and government offices will close for the day between approximately 2:00pm and 4:00pm
