Do you offer parking for non-hotel guests
<-answer->
The Guests visiting the Hotel Guests or the Day Visitors can also avail complimentary Valet Parking Services upon spending and presenting a bill of AED150.00 or above on the hotel services only.  We also offer complimentary parking for Day Visitors at the P18 parking area. When driving up The Palm towards Atlantis, please take a first right at the roundabout before you reach the Atlantis, The Palm building. A shuttle bus will pick our guests from the branded bus hop-on/ hop-off area and drop them off at the Avenues entrance from where they will be able reach the Aquaventure Ticketing Plaza.
