What's the price for the lost chambers aquarium
<-answer->
The admission ticket at the counter depends on the seasonality, however, the prices online is AED100 per adult and AED75 per child. UAE Residents get a special rate of AED75 per adult and AED50 per child ages 3 - 11 years old. All guests must present their original UAE Resident ID at the time of admission.  Children 03 – 11 years are subject to the child rate while children 0 – 02 years are given complimentary access.
