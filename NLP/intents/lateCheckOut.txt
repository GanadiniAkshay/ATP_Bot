How can I get a late check out
<-answer->
The Late Check-out request can be made at the time of booking the room/s or can be check and requested on the day. However, it is strictly subject to availability and incur additional charge.  Deluxe Rooms – AED125 per hour per room Imperial Club/Suites – AED175 per hour per room with an option to use the Imperial Club lounge From 21:00hrs onwards, the guest will be charged for full day. Keys – Guest will need to approach the Front Desk and request for a new key
