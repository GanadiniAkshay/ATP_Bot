What's included in your Half Board Package
<-answer->
Half Board package includes Hotel Stay, Breakfast and Dinner. Guests booked under Half Board are also entitled for a 50% discount on food only for lunch excluding Aquaventure restaurants and Friday & Saturday brunches in the resort. Few Restaurants included in Half Board for dinner require a further supplement to be paid per person depending upon the Restaurant. There are few restaurants that are not included in Half Board.
